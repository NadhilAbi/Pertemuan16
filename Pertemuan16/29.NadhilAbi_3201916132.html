Sinopsis Film The Gangster The Cop And The Devil

The Gangster, The Cop, The Devil is a crime action film about two men who work together to find a serial killer.

The man named Jang Dong-soo "The Gangster" (Ma Dong-seok) is a big gang boss, mafia and a thug who is not afraid of anyone.

Jang Dong-soo coincidentally encounters a serial killer named Kang Kyung-Ho “The Devil” (Kim Sung-Kyu). Kang Kyung-Ho "The Devil" tried to kill Jang Dong-soo by stabbing him several times.

However, Jang Dong-soo who was too strong made Kang Kyung-Ho fail to kill him. Having failed, Kang Kyung-hoo then fled the scene. Meanwhile, Jang Dong-soo who survived was eventually hospitalized and was rescued.

Due to the incident, Jang Dong-soo held a grudge and anger towards the man who tried to kill him.

Meanwhile, another man named Detective Jung Tae-seok "The Cop" (Kim Moo-yul) connects the dots of several recent murder cases and finds out that Jang Dong-soo is also a victim of The Devil. Detective Jung Tae-seok finally meets and persuades Jang Dong-soo to partner in finding serial killer Kyang Kyung-ho. Jang Dong-su has to work with Detective Jung Tae-seok, two different backgrounds, one criminal and the other a police officer.

However, various investigations were carried out with no results, both the police and the gang leader even though they had the same mission. How will the story continue? Can Jang Dong-su and Jung Tae-seok be able to find this mysterious figure? Who exactly is the figure of the mysterious serial killer?
